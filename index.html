About WoW

WoW (Wellbeing on Wheels) ensures everyone’s physical, mental and overall wellbeing by offering mobile, accessible wellness services. We run personalised fitness classes, group sessions, mental health counselling, and workshops. Our mission is simple: make wellbeing convenient and inclusive — and to create measurable social impact while doing it.

Social Impact

WoW believes wellbeing is a public good. Our social impact initiatives focus on making wellness accessible to underserved communities — schools, elderly homes, and low-income neighbourhoods. We run free or subsidized camps, train local volunteers as wellness ambassadors, and partner with NGOs to track long-term outcomes.

Community Wellness Camps

Monthly camps with screening, group therapy, fitness sessions and follow-ups. Measurable outcomes include improved mood scores and increased physical activity levels.

Volunteer Training

Short certification workshops for community volunteers so they can sustain wellness practices locally.

School Programs

Age-appropriate physical activity and mental-health curricula for schools, delivered in partnership with educators.

Impact Tracking

We collect simple pre/post metrics (surveys, attendance, basic fitness benchmarks) to evaluate and improve programs.
